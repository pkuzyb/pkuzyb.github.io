Hi! I am a phonetician and cognitive scientist. I work on phonology, phonetics, speech prosody, intonation, tone, and register, with a current focus on Western/Southern African and Sino-Tibetan languages. I also work on psycholinguistics, focusing on cognitive mechanisms in speech production and perception. My research methods combine behavioral experiments, computational modeling, and linguistic fieldwork. As a field linguist and educator, I am dedicated to developing accessible linguistic resources well-annotated speech databases and multimodal multimodal pedagogical materials that benefit the communities. My current research is on a dynamical gestural approach to tonal, respiratory, and supraglottal aspects of sentence intonation production in African languages. My hobbies are cooking, taking pictures and making videos.
